BSBot

Commands

Add Player [player]
Adds a player with the name of [player]
[quantity (1-4)], [card (1-13)]
Puts cards down for the current player
BS, [player1], [player2], [true/false]
Tells BSBot that [player1] called BS on [player2] and did they get the cards? [true/false]
PB, [player]
Tells BSBot that [player] is lying
cards, [player]/stack
Find out how the cards that a player has or are in the main stack
